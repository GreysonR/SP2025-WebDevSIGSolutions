Turkey, Cheddar, and Pickled Apple Sandwiches

Pickled apple made a sweet and tangy addition to grilled turkey and cheese sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 1c apple cider vinegar
  • 5T honey
  • 3/4t table salt
  • 1 apple
  • 1/2c mayonnaise
  • 1/4c mustard
  • 2t rosemary
  • 8 slices multigrain bread
  • 8 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1lb thinly sliced smoked turkey

Instructions

  1. Adjust oven rack to middle position and heat oven to 425 degrees. Microwave vinegar, ¼ cup honey, an teaspoon salt in bowl until bubbling, about 3 minutes. Stir in apple let stand for 5 minutes. Drain apple (brine can be saved and reused up to 2 more times).
  2. Whisk ¼ cup mayonnaise, mustard, rosemary, remaining 1 tablespoon honey, and remaining ¼ teaspoon salt in bowl. Spread 1 side of each slice of bread with remaining ¼ mayonnaise. Flip bread and distribute mayonnaise-mustard mixture slices. Distribute 4 slices of cheddar among 4 slices of bread, followed turkey; apple; remaining 4 slices of cheddar; and remaining 4 slices bread, mayonnaise side up.
  3. Transfer sandwiches to rimmed baking sheet and bake until cheddar is melted, about 15 minutes, flipping sandwiches halfway through baking. Serve.
